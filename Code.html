Kamal Oudghiri

At 39, this native of Fez is one of the people in charge of space communication for NASA in the center of Pasadena. This is how he supervised the links with the robots Spirit and Opportunity, sent to Mars, and those of the Cassini mission. Currently, he is working on communications with the future space station that NASA plans to install on the moon. Open and generous, he also strives to build bridges between Western scientists and Africa for the benefit of children.

  • Kamel El Oudghiri is a Moroccan communications engineer and astronaut from Figuig. He works for the US space agency, NASA. Al-Awdaghiri was among the crew supervising the landing of the two spacecraft Spirit and Opportunity on Mars, and also participated in the process of landing the Mars Science Laboratory, and his mission in this last mission was to follow the signals issued by the Curiosity rover during the process of its descent on Mars, he moved to Los Angeles, United States in the late eighties To complete his postgraduate studies in communications at the University of Southern California. Once again, Moroccan scientist Kamel Oudgiri marked a new achievement by being awarded the "Exceptional Service" medal by NASA.
